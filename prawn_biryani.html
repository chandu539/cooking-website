Prawn Biryani Recipe

Home Back to Biryani
Prawn Biryani is a delicious seafood dish that combines juicy prawns with fragrant basmati rice and a blend of spices. This biryani is perfect for seafood lovers who enjoy a flavorful and aromatic meal. The prawns are marinated and cooked to perfection, infusing the rice with a rich taste of the sea.

Ingredients

  • 500g Prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 2 large Onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 4 Green Chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup Mint Leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves, chopped
  • Whole Spices (Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom, Bay Leaf)
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate the prawns with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee and add the whole spices. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Then, add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add green chilies, mint, and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  5. Add the marinated prawns and cook for 5 minutes on low heat.
  6. In another pot, boil water, add salt, and cook the basmati rice until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  7. Layer the rice over the prawn mixture. Drizzle saffron milk and ghee over the top.
  8. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  9. Once done, fluff the rice gently with a fork. Serve hot with raita and salad.

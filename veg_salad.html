Veg Salad Recipe

Home Back to Salads
This Veg Salad is a fresh and healthy mix of colorful vegetables, perfect for a light meal or a nutritious side dish. Packed with vitamins and minerals, it's both delicious and good for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Red Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Herbs for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the chopped vegetables.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss well to coat.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve immediately.

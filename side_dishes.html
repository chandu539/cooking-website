Delicious Side Dishes

Explore our collection of mouthwatering side dishes to complement your main courses!

Butter Naan
Soft and fluffy Indian flatbread brushed with butter, perfect for soaking up rich curries and gravies.
Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Yogurt
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
Preparation Time:

2 hours (includes dough rising time)

Serving Suggestions:

Best served with butter chicken or paneer tikka masala.

Curd Rice
A comforting South Indian dish made with rice and yogurt, often tempered with spices and garnished with coriander.
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Yogurt
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Curry Leaves
  • Green Chilies
  • Salt
Preparation Time:

30 minutes

Serving Suggestions:

Enjoy with pickle and papadam.

Jeera Rice
Aromatic basmati rice cooked with cumin seeds and ghee, a simple yet flavorful side dish.
Ingredients:
  • Basmati Rice
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Ghee
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Water
Preparation Time:

25 minutes

Serving Suggestions:

Perfect with dal makhani or any curry.

Mirchi Bajji
Spicy green chilies stuffed with tangy masala and deep-fried in a chickpea flour batter. A popular Indian snack.
Ingredients:
  • Green Chilies
  • Chickpea Flour
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spices
Preparation Time:

40 minutes

Serving Suggestions:

Serve with tamarind chutney or yogurt.

Papad
Crispy, thin crackers made from lentil flour, often enjoyed as an accompaniment to meals.
Ingredients:
  • Lentil Flour
  • Spices
  • Oil
Preparation Time:

10 minutes

Serving Suggestions:

Enjoy as a crunchy side with any Indian meal.

Salad
A fresh mix of vegetables, dressed with a tangy vinaigrette, perfect for a light and healthy addition to any meal.
Ingredients:
  • Mixed Greens
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Carrot
  • Vinaigrette
Preparation Time:

15 minutes

Serving Suggestions:

Pair with grilled meats or as a refreshing side dish.

Samosa
Crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas, a favorite Indian street food and appetizer.
Ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Peas
  • Spices
  • Pastry Dough
  • Oil
Preparation Time:

1 hour

Serving Suggestions:

Serve with mint chutney or tamarind chutney.

Tamarind Rice
Tangy rice dish flavored with tamarind, peanuts, and spices. A flavorful and zesty South Indian classic.
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Tamarind
  • Peanuts
  • Spices
  • Mustard Seeds
Preparation Time:

45 minutes

Serving Suggestions:

Enjoy with yogurt or raita.

Tomato Rice
Spicy and tangy rice dish cooked with tomatoes, onions, and spices, often enjoyed with a side of raita.
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Coriander
Preparation Time:

30 minutes

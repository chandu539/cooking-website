Egg Biryani Recipe

Home Back to Biryani
Egg Biryani is a flavorful and rich dish that combines spiced eggs with fragrant basmati rice. This recipe is easy to make and offers a satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion. The eggs are boiled, fried, and then cooked with aromatic spices and basmati rice to create a delicious one-pot dish.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 2 large Onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 4 Green Chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup Mint Leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves, chopped
  • Whole Spices (Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom, Bay Leaf)
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil the eggs, peel them, and set aside.
  2. Fry the boiled eggs in hot oil until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  3. In a large pot, heat ghee and add the whole spices. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Then, add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add green chilies, mint, and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  6. Add the fried eggs to the mixture and cook for 5 minutes on low heat.
  7. In another pot, boil water, add salt, and cook the basmati rice until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  8. Layer the rice over the egg mixture. Drizzle saffron milk and ghee over the top.
  9. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Once done, fluff the rice gently with a fork. Serve hot with raita and salad.

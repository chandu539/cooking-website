Puri Recipe

Home Back to Breakfast
Puri is a deep-fried Indian bread that is fluffy and crispy. Itâ€™s a popular choice for breakfast or festive occasions and pairs wonderfully with curries and potato dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup Semolina
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tbsp Oil (for dough)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for frying)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, semolina, baking powder, and salt. Add oil and mix well.
  2. Gradually add water and knead to form a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle.
  5. Fry the puris in hot oil until puffed up and golden brown on both sides.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with your favorite curry or side dish.

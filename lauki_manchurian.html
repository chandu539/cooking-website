Lauki Manchurian Recipe

Home Back to Starters
Lauki Manchurian is a creative Indo-Chinese dish featuring bottle gourd (lauki) in a savory, spicy sauce. This dish combines the mild flavor of lauki with bold, tangy Manchurian spices for a unique and delicious experience.

Ingredients

  • 300g Lauki (Bottle Gourd), grated
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Sauce
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 small Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small Capsicum, finely chopped
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp Spring Onions, chopped
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix the grated lauki with cornflour and all-purpose flour. Form small fritters and deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© chopped onions, capsicum, and green chilies until they soften.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, tomato ketchup, and red chili sauce. Mix well.
  4. Add the fried lauki fritters to the sauce and toss gently to coat.
  5. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

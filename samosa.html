Samosa Recipe

Home Back to Snacks
Samosa is a popular Indian snack that features a crispy pastry filled with a spicy mixture of potatoes, peas, and spices. It's a favorite at parties and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Ghee or Oil
  • 2 large Potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup Green Peas
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix flour with ghee or oil to make a dough. Cover and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add peas and spices. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add mashed potatoes and mix well. Adjust seasoning and set the filling aside to cool.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out into thin circles.
  5. Cut each circle into half and form a cone. Fill with the potato mixture and seal the edges.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry the samosas until golden brown. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

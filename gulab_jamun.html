Gulab Jamun Recipe

Home Back to Desserts
Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian sweet made from milk solids, fried, and soaked in a fragrant rose-flavored syrup. Itâ€™s a rich and delicious dessert that's perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Milk Powder
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup Ghee
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 1 tsp Rose Water
  • Oil (for frying)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine milk powder, flour, and baking powder. Add ghee and milk, and mix until a smooth dough forms.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls, ensuring they are smooth and free of cracks.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the dough balls until golden brown, making sure they cook evenly.
  4. In a separate pan, combine sugar, water, and rose water. Bring to a boil, then simmer until the syrup thickens slightly.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the warm syrup for at least 30 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.

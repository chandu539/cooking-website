Rasmalai Recipe

Home Back to Desserts
Rasmalai is a delectable Indian dessert made from creamy milk, flavored with cardamom, and served with soft, spongy paneer balls. This dish is a perfect blend of sweetness and richness, ideal for festive occasions and special treats.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter Milk
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Paneer (or chenna)
  • 1/4 cup Cream
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 2 tbsp Rose Water
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • 1/4 cup Saffron Threads (soaked in warm milk)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil milk in a large pan and reduce it to about half its quantity. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  2. In a separate pan, cook paneer with some water until it becomes soft. Mash it to form a smooth dough.
  3. Form the paneer into small balls and gently add them to the boiling milk. Cook for a few minutes until they become soft and absorb the milk.
  4. Add cream, cardamom powder, rose water, and saffron to the milk. Stir well and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool. Garnish with chopped nuts before serving.

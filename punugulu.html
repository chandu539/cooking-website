Punugulu Recipe

Home Back to Breakfast
Punugulu are crispy South Indian fritters made from fermented idly or dosa batter, flavored with onions and spices. They are perfect as a tea-time snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Fermented Idly/Dosa Batter
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Onions
  • 1-2 Green Chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Coriander Leaves
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the fermented batter with chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok for deep frying.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  4. Remove the fried punugulu and drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.

Mirchi Bajji Recipe

Home Back to Snacks
Mirchi Bajji is a popular Indian snack made by dipping green chilies in a spicy chickpea flour batter and deep-frying them until crispy. It's a delicious and spicy treat perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Green Chilies
  • 1 cup Chickpea Flour
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Ajwain (Carom Seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Make a slit in each green chili and remove the seeds if you prefer less heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix chickpea flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, ajwain, and salt.
  3. Add enough water to make a thick batter that coats the chilies well.
  4. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Dip each chili into the batter and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

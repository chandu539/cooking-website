Butter Naan Recipe

Home Back to Side Dish
Butter Naan is a soft and fluffy Indian flatbread that is brushed with butter and perfect for soaking up rich curries and gravies. This traditional naan recipe will bring the taste of India right to your table.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Yeast
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Warm Water
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Milk (for brushing)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water and add yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture, yogurt, and melted butter.
  3. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rise for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 250°C (482°F) and place a baking sheet inside.
  5. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a thin oval shape.
  6. Place the rolled dough onto the hot baking sheet and bake for 5-7 minutes or until the naan puffs up and starts to brown.
  7. Brush the hot naan with melted butter and serve warm with your favorite curry or dish.

Tomato Rice Recipe

Home Back to Rice Dishes
Tomato Rice is a flavorful rice dish made with ripe tomatoes and a blend of spices. It's a delicious and comforting meal that's easy to prepare and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 large Tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 Green Chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped Coriander Leaves for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil starts to separate.
  3. Add the rice and garam masala. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add water as needed and cook until the rice is tender. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve warm.

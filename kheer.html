Kheer Recipe

Home Back to Desserts
Kheer is a traditional Indian rice pudding made with milk, sugar, and aromatic spices. This creamy and delicious dessert is often flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts, making it a delightful treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Basmati Rice
  • 1 liter Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • 1/4 cup Raisins
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tbsp Saffron Threads (soaked in warm milk)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a large pan and add the rice. Saut√© for a few minutes until the rice is slightly golden.
  3. Pour in the milk and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer, stirring frequently, until the rice is fully cooked and the milk has thickened (about 30-40 minutes).
  4. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron to the pan. Stir well to combine.
  5. In a separate small pan, toast the nuts and raisins in a bit of ghee until golden and add them to the kheer.
  6. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with additional nuts and saffron if desired.

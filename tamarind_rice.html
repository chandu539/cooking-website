Tamarind Rice Recipe

Home Back to Rice Dishes
Tamarind Rice is a tangy and spicy rice dish that is a staple in South Indian cuisine. It's made with tamarind pulp and a blend of spices, making it a flavorful and aromatic meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 1/4 cup Tamarind Pulp
  • 1 tbsp Chana Dal
  • 1 tbsp Urad Dal
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup Peanuts
  • 1/4 cup Curry Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, and peanuts. Sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and turmeric powder. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add tamarind pulp and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  5. Mix in the cooked rice and toss until well combined. Serve warm.

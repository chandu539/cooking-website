Idly Recipe

Home Back to Breakfast
Idly is a traditional South Indian steamed rice cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It's light, fluffy, and perfect for a healthy breakfast or snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1/4 cup Urad Dal (Black Lentils)
  • 1/2 tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Water (as needed)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and urad dal thoroughly. Soak them in water for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain and blend the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter. Add water as needed.
  3. Transfer the batter to a large bowl, cover, and let it ferment in a warm place for 12-24 hours.
  4. Once fermented, stir the batter and add salt.
  5. Pour the batter into idly molds and steam for 10-15 minutes or until cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

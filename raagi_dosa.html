Ragi Dosa Recipe

Home Back to Breakfast
Ragi Dosa is a nutritious and flavorful Indian crepe made from finger millet flour. It's a great choice for a healthy breakfast or snack, with its earthy taste and crisp texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Ragi Flour
  • 1/2 cup Rice Flour
  • 1/4 cup Urad Dal (soaked and ground)
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for cooking)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine ragi flour, rice flour, urad dal paste, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water to make a smooth batter. It should be slightly thicker than pancake batter.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle and brush it lightly with oil.
  4. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  5. Cook on medium heat until the edges start to lift and the bottom turns golden brown. Flip and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
